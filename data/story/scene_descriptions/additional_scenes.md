# Additional Scene Concepts (Future)

## 1) Ranger Station Archive
- **Mood:** Dusty vigilance; old systems still fighting.
- **Key Props:** Metal filing cabinets, analog weather monitor, pinned patrol maps, red-string board.
- **Hooks:** Discover permit anomalies; unlock Inês trust path; cross-reference symbol routes with legal boundaries.

## 2) Drained Marsh Edge
- **Mood:** Quiet alarm; nature missing in obvious ways.
- **Key Props:** Exposed roots, cracked mud, dead reed clusters, hidden survey stakes.
- **Hooks:** Ecological evidence sequence; optional sample collection puzzle; first direct proof of water diversion.

## 3) River Lock Maintenance Tunnel
- **Mood:** Mechanical claustrophobia; history repurposed for harm.
- **Key Props:** Rusted valve wheels, warning placards, floodgate key slot, dripping conduit.
- **Hooks:** Timed traversal under storm pressure; reveal underground cable nexus; climactic decision branch trigger.

## 4) Hilltop Fire Lookout (Abandoned)
- **Mood:** Melancholic perspective; beauty with consequence.
- **Key Props:** Broken binocular stand, wind logbook, signal lamp, cracked panoramic window.
- **Hooks:** End-of-act reflection scene; spot illegal transport lights at night; family reconciliation dialogue node.

## 5) Contractor Yard Perimeter (Optional Late-Game)
- **Mood:** Industrial secrecy; polished surfaces over dirty truth.
- **Key Props:** Numbered crates, fuel drums, tarp-covered drilling rigs, fake restoration signage.
- **Hooks:** Risk/reward stealth sequence; collect final corroborating documents; potential Mateus confrontation.
