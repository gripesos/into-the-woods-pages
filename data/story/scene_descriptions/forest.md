# Forest Scene Description

The forest begins as a place of wonder and gradually reveals procedural wounds. Ferns crowd narrow paths; trunks carry old scars beneath fresh cuts. Birdsong stutters in certain clearings, replaced by low-frequency vibration that players feel before they identify.

Key emotional tone: **awe turning to unease, then protective resolve**.

## Atmosphere Notes
- Layers of depth should suggest both invitation and concealment.
- Soundscape alternates between natural chorus and engineered interruptions.
- Mist and pollen motes make light beams visible, creating discoverable corridors.

## Interactive Focus
- Marked trees, hidden stakes, and disturbed soil as repeatable clue language.
- Crossroads where child intuition and technical logic both produce valid leads.
- Occasional safe micro-spaces (fallen log, stream bend) for family dialogue beats.

## Visual Style Cues (Retro Sierra + Ecological Mystery)
- Rich greens with sickly desaturated pockets near damaged zones.
- Distinct landmark silhouettes for navigation memory.
- Gentle parallax movement for canopy, vines, and fog.
