## Style + Tone Guardrails for Artists
- Prioritize **readability over visual noise** (classic adventure interaction clarity).
- Keep mystery **tender, not terrifying**.
- Use warm-vs-cool contrast to support emotional beats:
  - cabin scenes: warm_hearth/cozy_classic
  - investigation scenes: moonlit_mystery/twilight_whispers
- Child expressions should carry curiosity and vulnerability; avoid horror exaggeration.
- Environmental anomalies should feel ecological and plausible (patterns, growth, reflection), not supernatural VFX spam.
